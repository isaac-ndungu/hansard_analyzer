About Hansard Analyzer

Making Kenya's parliamentary records searchable, analyzable, and transparent to every citizen.

Hansard Analyzer is a comprehensive platform for exploring Kenya's parliamentary debates. It processes official Hansard records from the National Assembly and makes them fully searchable, allowing citizens, researchers, and journalists to track what their representatives say, follow legislation, and understand how parliament has debated Kenya's most important issues.

Instead of sifting through hundreds of PDF files, you can search speeches by keyword, browse individual MPs' records, explore how topics have evolved over time, and discover patterns in parliamentary discourse.

Key Features

Session Records

Browse complete transcripts from every National Assembly sitting, organized by date with speech counts and topics discussed

MP Profiles

View detailed profiles for every member, including participation trends, sentiment analysis, and topic focus areas

Topic Explorer

Track specific topics like healthcare, education, or infrastructure to see how they've been debated over time

Advanced Search

Search by keywords, date range, and other filters to find exactly what you're looking for

Analytics

See speaking frequency, word counts, sentiment patterns, and participation trends for members and topics

Sentiment Analysis

Understand the tone of parliamentary debates with automatic sentiment classification

Data Source

Hansard Analyzer processes official Hansard records published by the Kenya National Assembly. These are the official parliamentary transcripts containing complete records of debates, motions, and proceedings from the chamber.

Our goal is to make this public information more accessible and useful to everyone. While Hansard records are public, they're often hard to search and analyze. This platform bridges that gap.
